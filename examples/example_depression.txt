I can't remember the last time I actually felt okay
It's been months of the same thing every single day. I wake up exhausted no matter how long I sleep, and getting out of bed feels like the hardest part of the day. Things I used to enjoy, like drawing and going out with friends, just feel empty now, and I keep cancelling plans because I can't face anyone. My therapist says I should try to keep a routine but even small chores pile up until they feel impossible. I feel like a burden to everyone around me and I honestly don't see the point most days. I just want to feel something like hope again but I don't know how to get there.
