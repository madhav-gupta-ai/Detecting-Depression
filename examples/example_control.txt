Finally tried making sourdough from scratch and I get the hype now
So after two years of seeing everyone post their loaves online I finally caved and made a starter last month. The first two bakes were honestly bricks, dense enough to build a house with, but yesterday's loaf came out with an actual open crumb and the crust crackled when it cooled. I think the trick was being patient with the bulk ferment instead of rushing it. My kitchen smells amazing and my coworkers have already put in requests. Anyone have a good recipe for using up the discard? I have a jar of it staring at me and I feel bad throwing it out.
